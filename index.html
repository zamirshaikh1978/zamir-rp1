Description

A hot cup of tea spiced with freshly grated ginger, brightened with a dash of milk is how I love to start my day. This soothing and invigorating Indian-style Ginger tea, or Adrak wali chai, is made with just four ingredients, and is ready in 10 minutes!

Ingredients

  • Ginger
  • Cardamom
  • Indian Tea (loose tea or tea bags)
  • Milk

Directions

  1. Place a sauce pan on medium-high heat. Add water, grated ginger, gently crushed cardamom pods and let the water come up to a simmer.
  2. Add tea leaves (or tea bags) and bring everything to a roaring boil.
  3. Add milk and bring the tea to a full boil again. Turn off the heat.
  4. Using a fine mesh strainer, pour the tea in two cups. Add sugar or honey to taste, if using. Enjoy!
  5. Enjoy!!
